Android 15

Android 15 has officially arrived. Google's next-gen operating system is now available in the Android Open Source Project, with support for Pixel phones rolling out in the coming weeks. If you have a compatible device from Samsung, Motorola, OnePlus, Nothing, or other brands, you'll need to wait a bit longer, as Google expects Android 15 to arrive for those devices in the coming months.

The Remarkable Paper Pro

The Remarkable Paper Pro is the ultimate luxury
E-Ink note-taking device, reminiscent of flashy sports cars that turn heads but may not be practical. With a stunning 11.8-inch color display, front light, and the best keyboard case available, it excels at marking up documents and taking notes. Priced from $579, it’s a high-end choice that pushes E-Ink technology to its limits, making it a standout for serious users.
