How to Optimize Your Computer

  1. Uninstall unnecessary programs
  2. Delete temporary files and clear your cache
  3. Defragment your hard drive (for HDDs)
  4. Update your operating system and drivers
  5. Run antivirus and anti-malware scans

How to Set Up a Home Network

  1. Choose a router that fits your needs
  2. Position your router in a central location
  3. Secure your network with a strong password
  4. Set up your devices to connect to the network
  5. Consider setting up a guest network for visitors

How to Protect Your Online Privacy

  1. Use strong, unique passwords for each account
  2. Enable two-factor authentication where possible
  3. Be cautious about what you share on social media
  4. Use a VPN when on public Wi-Fi networks
  5. Regularly update your software and apps
